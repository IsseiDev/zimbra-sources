Managing Your Zimlets

Zimlets are add-on applications that enhance the functionality of your client. When a Zimlet is added to ZWC, you can look at information and interact with the third-party application from within your email messages.

Several pre-configured, default Zimlets are included when @ClientNameShort@ is installed. You can enable optional Zimlets from the Zimlet page, add Zimlets from the Zimbra Gallery, or create customized Zimlets so that you can interact with your company resources or other defined applications from the @ClientNameLong@.

There are Zimlets that are included in the @ClientNameLong@, and considered default Zimlets.

In addition to default Zimlets, you can download and deploy Zimlets from the Zimlet Gallery located on the Zimbra web site.

Default Zimlets

Default Zimlets are automatically enabled.

Option

Description

Phone

Highlights phone numbers to enable Skype calls.

URL Links

Highlights Web URLs for linking in email messages.

Email Contact Details

Highlights and previews associated contact details for an email address.

Date

Highlights dates, previews associated appointments and creates a link to the calendar.

Yahoo! Emoticons

Displays Yahoo! Emoticons images in email messages.

Drag-n-Drop Attachments

Provides you with the ability to drag-n-drop file attachments when composing an email.

Email Attacher

Attach email messages when composing a new message.

Attach Contacts

Allows attaching contacts when composing a new message.

Search Highlighter

After a mail search, this Zimlet highlights Search terms with yellow color.

Secure Email

Sign, verify, encrypt, and decrypt emails with S/MIME.

WebEx

Easily schedule start or join WebEx meetings.
